# Introduction

This project **Adidas Sales Analysis** report is designed to analyze sales performance and identify revenue trends, customer demand patterns, and key growth opportunities using data analysis techniques.

The report explores:
- Performance Analysis 
- Regional and Temporal Trends 
- Profitability Insights
- Product-Specific Analysis

 The purpose of this analysis is to help business teams make informed decisions by clearly showing sales trends, top-performing products and regions, and specific areas that need improvement, in order to improve revenue, pricing decisions, and overall business strategy for Adidas.

 # Problem Statement
The **Addias store** facing difficulty clearly understanding which products, sales channels, regions, and time periods drive the most revenue and profit, making it hard to optimize sales performance, pricing, and inventory decisions. 

This project addresses this challenges by transforming raw retail transaction records into meaningful insights through the use of **PowerBI.** The analysis highlights Performance Analysis, Regional and Temporal Trends, Profitability Insights and Product-Specific Analysis.

# Key Posing Business Questions

To maximize strategic value, this analysis was framed from the perspective of retail decision-makers such as the Sales Director, Marketing Manager, and Store Operations Lead. The following questions guided the KPIs, measures, and dashboard design:

- What is the total revenue generated by each product category (e.g., Men's Street Footwear, Women's Apparel)?
- Which sales method (In-store vs. Outlet) generates the highest revenue across all products?
- What is the average operating margin across all product categories?
- How does the total revenue change across different weeks or months?
- Which region contributes the most to the total sales, and does it align with the highest profitability?
- Are there specific dates or weeks when certain products experienced a significant spike or drop in sales?
- Which product category has the highest operating profit across both sales methods?
- What is the average profit margin for products sold via In-store vs. Outlet methods?
- Which product had the lowest operating margin, and how can this be improved?
- What is the total revenue generated from each product in the Men's Footwear category?
- How does the performance of Women's Athletic Footwear compare to Women's Street Footwear in terms of sales and profit?
- What is the average units sold per day for each product category?

# Dataset Overview

This dataset contains **9648 Total Transactions** it captures detailed informations like Retailer, Retailer ID, Region,State, City, Product, Per unit price, Unit sold,Total sales, Operating profit,Operating margin,Sales Method,Sales price, Profit margin,Cost and Adidas Calender, making it suitable for analysis.
   
 - **Retailer**- The retailer stores that sells products directly to consumers( Warmart, Amazon, Foot locker, Kohl's, Sport direct, Wear gear). 
 - **Retailer ID**- Unique identifer for each customers.
 - **Region**- The geographical area in the US in which the products are sold.
 - **State**- The states in which the products are sold.
 - **City**- The cites in which the products are sold.
 - **Product**- The various types of products sold.
 - **Per unit price**- The amount of money charged to a customer for a single product.
 - **Unit sold**- The total quantity of products sold during a specific period.
 - **Total sales**- The total money received from selling the product.
 - **Operating profit**- The profit earned before interest and task.
 - **Operating margin**- The percentage of revenue that remains as operating profit after covering all operating expenses.
 - **Sales Method**- The method in which the products are sold(Out-let, Online, In-store).
 - **Sales price**- The final price which was paid for the product.
 - **Profit margin**- The percentage ratio that shows how much revenue is kept as profit after expenses.
 - **Cost**- The total expense incurred to produce, acquire, and deliver the product.
 - **Addidas Calender** The period in which the trasaction occurred, this took the span of 2 year(2020,2021).
